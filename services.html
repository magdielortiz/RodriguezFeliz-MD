Fly In Program

Dr. Rodríguez-Feliz offers a Fly in Program to patients who do not live in the Miami Beach Area. The program offers assistance with organizing the trip, traveling details and transportation. If the patient is found to be a good candidate, he or she will be offered a private virtual consult or private phone consult after having submitted the proper information needed for the procedure of their choice.

Financing

There are two ways to finance a procedure with Dr. Rodríguez-Feliz. With CareCredit, a health credit card that helps you pay for out-of-pocket healthcare expenses for you or your family, and with Alphaeon Credit; a credit card that allows patients to provide financing to Board Certified Plastic Surgeons and Dermatologists.
